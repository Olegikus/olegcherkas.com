Monthly LinkedIn outreach management

A monthly service for founders and B2B firms that want LinkedIn outreach handled consistently, from targeting and messages to follow-ups, reply tracking and weekly reporting.

Ongoing outreach, not one-time setup

Many firms start LinkedIn outreach and stop after a few days because nobody has time to manage the process. The real value comes from consistent targeting, follow-ups, reply review and weekly optimization.

We manage the monthly outreach process for your profile or your company team, keeping the activity professional, controlled and focused on real business conversations.

What we manage

Monthly LinkedIn outreach management: target list updates, Sales Navigator searches, connection requests, follow-up messages, reply tracking, lead qualification and simple CRM or spreadsheet reporting.

This is best for founders, law firms, accounting firms, recruiters, consultants and B2B service companies that want LinkedIn to run as a steady client acquisition channel.

Our process

01
Monthly targeting

We update the target audience, search criteria and lead lists based on your current business focus.

02
Outreach activity

We manage connection requests and follow-up activity using a controlled and professional outreach process.

03
Message optimization

We improve messages based on replies, objections and the type of clients you want to attract.

04
Reply tracking

We monitor replies, highlight positive responses and help move relevant prospects toward a call.

05
Weekly reporting

You receive a weekly update with activity, replies, positive conversations and next actions.
