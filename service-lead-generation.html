Service 01

LinkedIn lead generation setup

A clear setup for companies that want to start generating B2B leads through LinkedIn without guessing whom to target, what to say, or how to structure the first campaign.

Why targeting matters

We define who you should contact, where to find them, and how to start conversations in a professional way. This includes your ideal client profile, target industries, company size, seniority, geography and Sales Navigator filters.

The goal is to give your firm a practical LinkedIn lead generation foundation that can be launched, measured and improved without turning your profile into a spam machine.

What you get

A ready-to-launch LinkedIn lead generation setup: ICP definition, Sales Navigator search logic, qualified lead list structure, connection request copy, follow-up messages and a simple launch plan.

Ideal for law firms, accounting firms, consultants, recruiters and B2B service companies that want to test LinkedIn as a client acquisition channel before committing to monthly management.

Our process

01
ICP and target list

We define your ideal client profile and build a qualified prospect list using Sales Navigator.

02
Message scripts

We write connection requests and follow-up messages for your specific audience and market.

03
Campaign launch plan

We prepare a simple plan for sending connection requests, following up and tracking replies.

04
Reply handling logic

We define how to qualify replies, when to continue the conversation and when to move someone to a call.

05
Basic reporting

You get a simple reporting structure for connections, replies, positive responses and booked calls.
