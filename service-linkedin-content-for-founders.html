LinkedIn content for founders

LinkedIn posts written for founders, partners and business owners who want to stay visible, build trust and explain their expertise without sounding like generic marketing content.

Content that supports trust

For lawyers, accountants, consultants and B2B founders, LinkedIn content is not about going viral. It is about staying visible to the right people and showing that you understand their business problems.

We build content around your real experience, your clients, your market and your point of view. The goal is to make your profile more credible before calls, referrals and outreach conversations.

What we deliver

A monthly LinkedIn content plan with posts written in your voice, covering topics that matter to your target clients. We handle ideation, writing, formatting and publishing guidance.

The content can support outbound, referrals and inbound trust by making your expertise visible before someone speaks with you.

Our process

01
Content audit

We review your current LinkedIn content and identify what sounds generic, unclear or inconsistent.

02
Positioning angle

We define your main themes based on your expertise, services, market and target clients.

03
Monthly content plan

We build a practical content plan with topics, post angles and publishing rhythm.

04
Post writing

We write posts in your voice so your profile sounds like a real founder, not a marketing agency.

05
Monthly review

We review engagement, conversations and content direction, then adjust the plan for the next month.
