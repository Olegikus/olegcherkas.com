Common questions

How does the first call work?

It's a free 30-minute call with no agenda other than understanding your situation. I'll ask about your target market, current pipeline and goals - and you'll leave with at least one concrete idea you can act on immediately.

Do you work with companies outside GCC?

Yes. My strongest market experience is across GCC and Australia, but the same LinkedIn profile, content and outreach principles can be adapted for other B2B service markets. The key is having a clear offer, a defined target audience and a professional sales process.

Do you do the outreach yourself or teach me?

Both, depending on what you need. Some clients want done-for-you execution, others want to learn the system and run it themselves. We figure out the right fit on the first call.

How quickly can we start?

We can usually start within 1 week after the first call. First meaningful data usually appears within 2-3 weeks, with qualified conversations and meetings typically following within 4-6 weeks.
